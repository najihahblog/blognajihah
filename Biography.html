BIOGRAPHY

I am Farah Najihah, just call me Najihah. I'm 22 years old and born on the 20th day of Febuary year 2001.Iam the 3rd child among 3rd children of my parents. Nobody comes in this world, without the support of family and friends. Actually, whatever you will be, it is just because of your family. They both love their occupation. That’s I have learned from my parents the value of time, honesty, hard work and commitment to the purpose. I have two sisters. Being the youngest child gets everything. I could not agree any less with this phrase when I was the youngest in my family. We all are in the same school. We live in Kepong Kuala Lumpur.

Iam a 2nd year student University Technology MARA (UiTM) Shah Alam. Growing up I was always interested in the sciences and technology. In high school, I excelled in math classes, which led me to pursue a degree in Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons.) Netcentric Computing. I intend to work in my subject of study once I've earned my degree. I'm thrilled to be a student at this fantastic university with its supportive and caring faculty.I have extraordinary skills in some subjects whereas I am very weak in the few.The advice of my parents had a keen effect on my habits. I make an effort not to lie and convey the truth as I see it. My parents have always told me that I should own up to my mistakes. I make an effort to do that. I am aware of how to be content in any circumstance. Since I firmly believe that "Happiness is not outside of you; it is within you." In contrast to doing the same things over and over, I enjoy doing creative things. One thing I always enjoy doing is learning new things. I keep up with the news every day
